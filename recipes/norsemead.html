Norse Mead Recipe

Main Description

Ingredients

  • An open-mouthed vessel at least a quart in size
  • A wooden stir stick
  • A clean cheesecloth, towel or t-shirt large enough to cover the opening of the vessel
  • 1/2 cup of raw, unfiltered honey
  • 2 cups spring water
  • Any organic fresh or dried berries, grapes or plums
  • A small bunch of any wild, unsprayed botanical such as wildflower petals

Recipe

  1. Mix honey and room-temperature water together thoroughly in your open-mouthed container
  2. Drop in dried or fresh berries and optional botanicals
  3. Place the vessel in a warm, dark corner (about 70 degrees F with no direct sunlight) and cover with a cloth
  4. You will want to return to the vessel at least three times daily to give a vigorous stir
  5. Meditate quietly, sing, chant, to invoke brewing spirits
  6. After five days rejoice and give last stir
  7. Drink to delight from container
