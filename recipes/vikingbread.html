Viking Bread Recipe

Main Description

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of whole wheat flour
  • 2 cups of all-purpose four
  • 1 tsp. of baking powder
  • 1 tsp. of salt
  • 2 cups of water
  • 3/4 cup of rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup of rolled oats for topping

Recipe

  1. Mix all dry ingredients(except topping) and then add water
  2. Stir all ingredients with a wooden spoon until you can't stir anymore
  3. Knead the dough with a large war hammer
  4. Form dough into a round shape
  5. Place onto baking stone and sprinkle topping oats
  6. Heat oven to 375 and cook for one hour
  7. Remove from oven and cut into pieces with axe
